# amazon-discounts-vs-ratings-analysis
An exploratory analysis about Amazon dataset available on Kaggle to analyse and understanding the relationship between price discounts and ratings.

This is part of Signal Processing for Data Science Assignment from my Bachelor of Science in International Business Information Systems at the Faculty Business Information Systems of the Furtwangen University of Applied Sciences.

Kaggle Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/karkavelrajaj/amazon-sales-dataset?resource=download
